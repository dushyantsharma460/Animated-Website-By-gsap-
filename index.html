About Us

Pokémon became one of the most successful video game franchises in the world, second only to Nintendo's Super Mario Bros. The original Pokémon is a role-playing game based around building a small team of monsters to battle other monsters in a quest to become the best.

Strongest Trainer

There are many trainers in Pokemon, but some are considered the best. In Pokemon history Ash Ketchum is the best trainer in the pokemon world.

Strongest Pokemon

It greatly depends on factors such as the Pokemon's type, abilities, level, and individual stats. However, some of the strongest Pokemon include Mewtwo, Charizard.

Best Gym Trainer
